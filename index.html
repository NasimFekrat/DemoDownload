Bio

Nasim Fekrat

With a robust blend of technical acumen and diverse professional experience, I was able to carved a niche as a proficient Full Stack Web Developer. My journey in technology began with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering from Azad University, Central Tehran Branch, Tehran, Iran, culminating in a pivotal final project on the Scrutiny and Implementation of Wireless Communication in Smart House. This project not only laid the foundation for my technical prowess but also ignited a passion for creating innovative solutions.
In March 2019, I augmented my skill set by completing a Full Stack Web Developer Course at the University of Toronto's SCS Coding Boot Camp. This rigorous program honed my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and Python, among others, setting my on a path to excellence in building responsive, user-centric web applications. I am particularly passionate about enhancing user experience and employs a meticulous approach to developing efficient web solutions that address complex problems and foster business growth.
Professionally, I embarked on my career as a Quality Assurance Analyst at Pong Game Studios, Woodbridge, ON, where I was instrumental in refining web-based projects through comprehensive testing from backend to frontend. I adept use of TestRail and Jira for test case management and my agility in adapting to client-server architectures underscored my capability to ensure product excellence.
My journey then led me to TD Bank in Markham, ON, where I served as an Underwriter. Here, I demonstrated my analytical prowess by making well-rationalized credit decisions, contributing significantly to the bank's risk management framework. My ability to prioritize and manage workloads effectively, coupled with a commitment to maintaining a culture of risk management, showcased my versatility beyond technical development roles.
Currently, I am leveraging my full spectrum of skills as a Full Stack Web Developer at Crosslinx Transit Solution in Toronto, ON. Since August 2023, I have been pivotal in developing a comprehensive web application for asset tracking, data storage, and reporting. My work, characterized by a blend of PHP, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, and the innovative use of Power BI for data visualization, has significantly enhanced operational efficiencies and informed decision-making processes. My commitment to implementing security best practices and my proactive approach to maintaining the application highlight my dedication to excellence and continuous improvement.
My technical skills are comprehensive, encompassing coding, server and database management, version control, and deployment tools. I excel in responsive and mobile-first design, Bootstrap, React, Node.js, RESTful API design and development, and is proficient in cloud platforms like Cloudways, as well as in tools such as Docker and Heroku. my expertise in MS Office, advanced Excel, and Power BI further enhances my ability to deliver insightful and impactful solutions.
Beyond my technical capabilities, I am a collaborator at heart, skilled in working within teams and utilizing platforms like Git and GitHub for version control and project management. My career trajectory is a testament to my adaptability, technical proficiency, and unwavering commitment to advancing the field of web development. I am not just a developer; I am a visionary, continually seeking to expand my knowledge and embrace emerging technologies to stay at the cutting edge of the industry.
